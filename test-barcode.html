Instructions

  1. Click "Open Scanner" to test the camera interface
  2. Use the test barcode buttons to simulate scanning known products
  3. On mobile devices, the camera should automatically use the rear camera
  4. The scanner supports various barcode formats (UPC, EAN, Code 128, etc.)

Troubleshooting

  • Camera not working: Ensure you've granted camera permissions
  • Barcode not detected: Make sure the barcode is well-lit and in focus
  • No nutrition data: Some products may not be in the OpenFoodFacts database
